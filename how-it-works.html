The biography allows the person to write a short summary of his or her own life. It has a limit of 2,000 characters including white spaces in which the person can type.

Resume

The resume gives the person the opportunity to display his or her life and work experiences. The resume has a limit of 4,000 characters including white spaces in which the person can type. It is strongly recommended that the person copy and paste his or her resume.
