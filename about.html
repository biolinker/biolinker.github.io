About

Concept & Idea

The idea is very simple. As the name implies, Contact Information Soical Media Biography Resume (CISMBR) is an all-in-one repository that allows a person to store his or her contact information, social medias hyperlinks, biography, and/or resume online as a static Web page for the world to see.

Who Is It For?

Employers & Job Seekers

The CISMBR can be beneficial for employers and job seekers. A job seeker, for example, can create his or her Contact Information Social Media Biography Resume static Web page that contains pertinent information such as contact information, social media account links, biography, and resume for employers to see information about the potential job candidate. In turn, the employer can visit the potential job candidate Contact Information Social Media Biography Resume's Web page to gather information about the potential job candidate in addition to viewing his or her related social media accounts. In essence, the Contact Informaiton Social Media Biography Resume acts like a bridge between employers and job seekers.

Other Users

While Contact Information Social Media Biography Resume is great for employers and job seekers, it can also be great for a person who wishes to create his or her biography and social media account links only static Web page. Contact Information Social Media Biography Resume gives the person the ability to toggle (show/hide) his or her social media, biography, and/or resume. In other words, the Contact Information Social Media Biography Resume can be a Web page made up of only social media, biography, and/or resume. For example, John Doe can create a biography and social media account links only Web page. Jane Doe, for example, can create a biography only Web page. There are many different combinations to choose from in Contact Information Social Media Biography Resune static Web page.
